Financial Aid


Princeton provides a generous amount of financial aid to both international and domestic students. Recent graduates report facing little to no debt after college.

Current Financial Aid Data As of 2025


Parental income cap for financial aid: $180,000/yr
Those with income <=$65,000/yr receives full coverage
Average grants: $62,200
Few seniors face up to $9,600 debt, while 83% seniors face none. #

Overview of Princeton's Financial Aid

  • Step 1: After you finish and submit your application to Princeton, fill out the Princeton Financial Aid Application (PFAA).
  • Step 2: After submitting the PFAA, you submit any supporting documentation as needed.
  • Step 3: Fill out any parental information as needed

Complete your FAFSA application (Princeton code: 002627) here. Visit Princeton’s Financial Aid page for important specifics, tips, and deadlines not disclosed.
