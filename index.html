About Princeton


Founded in 1746, Princeton is home to thousands of ambitious tigers. It also happens to be the fourth-oldest college in the US. As a prestigious liberal arts university, Princeton focuses on community service and offers wonderful opportunities for the many passions its students have, ranging from STEM research to reputable arts programs.
